If 1) you would like to be a moderator, or 2) you have just been this week's moderator and need to move yourself to the bottom of the list, or 3) wish to swap time with a colleague, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the DAWN website.
  2. Go to the Journal Club page.
  3. In the top bar, click Beaver Builder.
  4. Scroll down to the "List of moderators" accordion (the gray bar with blue text) and click this module.
  5. In the popup, click "Edit item" at the "List of moderator" item (which is the only item).
  6. In the new popup editor, choose the "Text" tab, rather than the "Visual" tab.
  7. You can now edit, add, delete, or move a row in the table. If you're not used to HTML code if might be a little confusing, but one row is enclosed in the <tr> and </tr> tags, and consists of three columns with <td>/</td> tags, as seen in the figure on the right.
  8. Finally, you click
    Save   → Save   → Done  → Submit for Review
