Comet P2007

This comet passed Earth in 2007, where I observed it from La Palma. Like all color images, this one is composed from three different images with different color filter. The telescope was set to follow the comet, which moves rather fast across the sky. During the few minutes between each exposure, it has moved a little, relative to the sky and the background stars. For that reason, the stars are seen as three dots in different colors, shifted a bit.
