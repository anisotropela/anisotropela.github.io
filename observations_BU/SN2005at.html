SN2005at supernova

This supernova exploded on March 15, 2005. I observed it every night during my stay at La Silla.

I will write more on this later on. For now, just enjoy the beautiful image of the supernova's host galaxy, NGC 6744, situated at a distance of 36 million lightyears.
